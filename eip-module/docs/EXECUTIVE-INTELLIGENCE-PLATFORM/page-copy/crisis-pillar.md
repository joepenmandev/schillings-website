## Hero

**Eyebrow.** Pillar — Crisis Intelligence

**Headline.** The first 72 hours, simulated and shaped.

**Sub-headline.** Crisis is no longer an event; it is a posture. The platform compresses forty years of Schillings practice into a structured 72-hour simulation, with explicit intervention leverage points and a board-grade Briefing PDF.

---

## What it answers

- If something happened tonight, what does the next 72 hours look like — and where is intervention leverage highest?
- Which stakeholders are likely to react first, and with what severity?
- What headlines are most plausible in the first 24 hours?
- What are the operational priorities, in order of urgency?

## How the Forecast is built

- **Windowed timeline.** 0-2h, 2-6h, 6-24h, 24-48h, 48-72h.
- **Stakeholder response map.** Calibrated to the principal's likely audiences.
- **Reputational severity index.** Banded composite with confidence.
- **Intervention leverage points.** Ranked operational moves with explicit framing as *non-legal* priorities.

1. **Run the Crisis Escalation Forecast** on the scenario the principal is preparing for or facing.

---

## Privacy and refusal

- Inputs are not retained beyond the session by default.
- The Forecast refuses to assess scenarios that target named individuals with assertions of wrongdoing.
- The Forecast refuses scenarios involving minors as subjects.
- The Forecast refuses scenarios that imply intent to retaliate or interfere with regulatory or judicial processes.
