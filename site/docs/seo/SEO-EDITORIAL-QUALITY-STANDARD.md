# Editorial quality standard (Phase 3A)

**Classification:** REVIEW REQUIRED (editorial, partnership, brand)  
**Purpose:** A **durable quality bar** for Intelligence — what “authoritative” means in practice — without prescribing a single house voice or heavy bureaucracy.

## What this protects

- **Tone** — premium, calm, confident; not homogenised.  
- **Depth** — insight over volume.  
- **Differentiation** — if it could run under any firm’s logo, it fails the bar.

This document does **not** enforce one writing style. It enforces **minimum authority signals**.

---

## A strong piece should ideally contain

Several of the following (not a mechanical checklist on every article):

| Element | Meaning |
|---------|--------|
| **Original framing** | Why this piece exists now — not a restatement of the obvious lede. |
| **Expert interpretation** | Named judgment: what the development *means* for sophisticated readers. |
| **Strategic implications** | Consequences for decisions, reputation, record, or posture — proportionate and bounded. |
| **Synthesis** | Connects threads others leave separate (law + narrative + security, etc.). |
| **Sophisticated positioning** | Respects reader intelligence; avoids patronising or panic framing. |
| **Insight density** | Each section earns its place; no filler scaffolding. |
| **Non-obvious perspective** | A reader finishes with at least one reframing they did not bring in. |
| **Calm authority** | Confidence without hype; precision without hedging everything away. |

---

## What a piece should avoid

| Pattern | Why it fails |
|---------|----------------|
| **Generic explainers** | Commodity content; weak citation and weak brand memory. |
| **Summary journalism** | Wire-grade value; better left to news outlets unless reframed with firm judgment. |
| **Keyword-led structure** | Sections exist for search terms, not for argument. |
| **SEO intro padding** | Throat-clearing and definitions to “hit” terms — reads as optimised. |
| **“Five things to know”** (as default shape) | Often listicle theatre; use only when the list is genuinely the insight. |
| **Traffic-blog formatting** | Artificial skimmability that replaces substance. |
| **Repetitive phrasing** | Same head-term variants — sounds engineered. |
| **AI-style filler** | Generic transitions, hollow balance, pattern-completed paragraphs. |
| **Surface-level commentary** | Obvious takes that add no institutional view. |

## Sophistication check (operational)

Before committing to publish, ask:

> *Would a sophisticated client, journalist, general counsel, adviser, or executive learn something **non-obvious** from this?*

If the honest answer is no, **do not publish** — or elevate until it passes (expert input, sharper framing, narrower scope, or kill).

## Relationship to expertise (commercial family)

Intelligence **supports** expertise through depth and credibility; it does **not** replace commercial pages. Body copy must not read as a disguised service landing page (see authority strategy and anchor vocabulary docs).
