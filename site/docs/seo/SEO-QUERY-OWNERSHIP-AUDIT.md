# SEO query ownership audit (Phase 2C)

**Classification:** REVIEW REQUIRED  
**Purpose:** Surface where **Google could misunderstand** commercial vs informational vs trust intent—and calibrate monitoring.  
**Companion docs:** [`SEO-AUTHORITY-OWNERSHIP-MAP.md`](SEO-AUTHORITY-OWNERSHIP-MAP.md), [`SEO-ANCHOR-VOCABULARY.md`](SEO-ANCHOR-VOCABULARY.md), [`SEO-MEASUREMENT-FRAMEWORK.md`](SEO-MEASUREMENT-FRAMEWORK.md).

**Legend — risk:** ambiguity that could cause wrong URL to own a commercial head term.  
**Legend — confidence:** how stable ownership is given current copy + internal graph.

## Cluster: Reputation, privacy, defamation, online harm

| Field | Value |
|--------|--------|
| **Primary owner URL** | `/expertise/reputation-privacy/` (all locales) |
| **Secondary support** | Situations: media scrutiny, reputation under threat, family privacy, online attacks & misinformation; partial: AI/deepfake |
| **Informational support** | Intelligence: topic archives (Reputation, Privacy, Disinformation, Smear campaigns, Online profiles) |
| **Neutral mentions** | People profiles (directory tags → hub); offices (contact/geo only) |
| **Prohibited competing surfaces** | Article URLs, people URLs, office URLs as **primary** for “reputation lawyers”-class commercial queries |
| **Expected SERP role (primary)** | Expertise hub: commercial / practice discovery |
| **Expected SERP role (support)** | Situations: scenario long-tail; Intelligence: informational; People: branded + E-E-A-T |
| **Current risk level** | **Medium** — editorial topics and scenarios can overlap vocabulary with hub |
| **Confidence** | **Established** for hub as primary; topic bridge + hub framing reduce drift |
