## Hero

**Eyebrow.** Pillar — Exposure Intelligence

**Headline.** The surface area of a modern principal.

**Sub-headline.** Public data. AI visibility. Family exposure. Executive traceability. Deepfake vulnerability. Search posture. Data-leak presence. Reputation attack surface. Eight signal categories, one composite Index.

---

## What it answers

- How exposed am I, really — across the open web, AI systems, and adjacent vectors?
- Where would a knowledgeable adversary start if they wanted to reconstruct my operating life?
- How attractive a target am I to AI-enabled impersonation?
- What changed in the last 90 days?

**Output.** Executive Exposure Index across eight named sub-indices. Confidence-banded, provenance-disclosed, optionally analyst-reviewed.

### AI Impersonation Exposure Audit

## How the Index is built

A short structured explainer:

- **Signals.** Eight named signal categories, each documented in the methodology.
- **Confidence.** Per signal, per sub-index, rolled up to the composite.
- **Adversary lens.** Optional rendering through Threat Actor archetypes.
- **Refusal patterns.** Documented limits on what the Index will and will not produce.

**CTA.** Read the Executive Exposure Index methodology (v1.0)

---

## Recommended journey

1. **Run the Executive Exposure Audit.** Establish the principal's posture.
2. **Open the Adversary Lens.** Re-render the posture through the most relevant archetype.
3. **Run the AI Impersonation Exposure Audit.** Quantify synthetic-media-enabled risk specifically.
4. **Consider analyst review.** A named Schillings partner returns signed observations within SLA.

---

## Privacy and refusal

- Inputs are not retained beyond the session by default.
- The Audit refuses to assess third parties without authorised-representative consent.
- The Audit refuses to assess minors, and to ingest sensitive personal data without consent.
