## What it answers

- How exposed am I across the open web, AI systems, and adjacent vectors?
- Which sub-indices are driving my posture?
- How attractive a target am I to AI-enabled impersonation?
- What is the single largest unknown about my profile?

---

## What it produces

The **Executive Exposure Index** rendered as a band (Low / Moderate / Elevated / High / Severe), composed of eight sub-indices:

- AI Visibility
- Public Exposure
- Family Exposure
- Executive Traceability
- Deepfake Vulnerability
- Search Index Exposure
- Data Leak Presence
- Reputation Attack Surface

Each sub-index is presented with band, score, contributing signals, and confidence.

---

## Inputs

- Full name (legal and commonly used variants).
- Current organisation and role.
- Email address (used only for delivery; not retained by default).
- Optional: linked profiles (LinkedIn, X, primary social).
- Optional: primary domain.
- Optional: jurisdictions.
- Optional: family-office consent flags.

The Audit refuses to ingest sensitive personal data without consent.

---

## What you receive

A unified Schillings Intelligence Report containing:

- Top-line summary and the two highest-contributing sub-indices.
- Sub-index breakdown.
- Adversary lens (optional rendering through Threat Actor archetypes).
- Confidence panel.
- AI provenance disclosure.
- Recommended priorities — three operational actions.
- Linked insights to related assessments.
- Analyst review CTA.

Free top-line scan; gated full Intelligence Report; optional analyst review by a named Schillings partner.

## How it connects

- → Open the **Adversary Lens** to re-render posture through a Threat Actor archetype.
- → Run the **AI Impersonation Exposure Audit** to quantify synthetic-media-enabled risk.
- → Run the **Headline Risk Assessment** if a specific communication is being prepared.
- → Run the **Crisis Escalation Forecast** if an incident is being modelled or anticipated.

---

## Privacy and refusal

- Inputs are not retained beyond the session by default.
- The Audit refuses to assess third parties without authorised-representative consent.
- The Audit refuses to assess minors.
- The Audit refuses sensitive personal data without consent.
