# Threat Actor Library — Adversary Archetypes

*Working draft. v0.1.*

The Threat Actor Library is a controlled taxonomy of adversary *archetypes*. Archetypes are never identifications of named individuals or organisations. They exist to make the platform's outputs predictive, specific, and operationally useful — without crossing the line into defamation or speculation about real actors.

Each archetype below is structured identically:

- **Archetype name** — short, named, evocative.
- **Operating mode** — how this archetype works in practice.
- **Primary motivations** — what it is optimising for.
- **Tactics** — the moves it tends to make.
- **Tells** — the early signals that suggest this archetype is in play.
- **Where it shows up** — which Indexes most often surface evidence of this archetype's activity.

The Library is owned by editorial and is updated quarterly. Each update is versioned.

---

## 1. Activist Coalition

**Operating mode.** A loose coalition of advocacy groups, researchers, and digital organisers who coordinate around a shared cause and a chosen public-facing target.

**Primary motivations.** Visibility for the cause; behavioural change in the target organisation or principal; recruitment of a wider audience.

**Tactics.** Coordinated framings; pre-built media packs; rapid social amplification; deliberate cultivation of investigative reporters; staged in-person actions designed for maximum visual capture.

**Tells.** Multi-account framings appearing in lockstep; a clean, consistent narrative across superficially unrelated outlets; coordinated timing windows.

**Where it shows up.** Narrative Escalation Map (Active to Amplifying vectors), Public Interpretation Analysis (Activist coalition lens), Crisis Escalation Forecast (24-72h windows).

---

## 2. Hostile State-Aligned Media Operator

**Operating mode.** A state-aligned outlet, content network, or amplification operation working to influence public perception in service of a foreign-policy or adversarial geostrategic goal.

**Primary motivations.** Strategic disruption; agenda dilution; long-horizon reputational damage to specific individuals or sectors.

**Tactics.** Fabricated documents and quotes; legitimate-looking but selectively edited footage; cultivated proxy outlets; long-tail social platform amplification; targeted information laundering through small or partisan outlets.

**Tells.** Sudden coverage in low-credibility outlets in unusual languages; quotes that cannot be sourced; footage with detectable provenance gaps; near-simultaneous appearance across geographically dispersed accounts.

**Where it shows up.** Narrative Escalation Map (Adversary lens), Executive AI Misuse Risk (synthetic-media scenarios), Crisis Escalation Forecast (where attribution is contested).

---

## 3. AI-Augmented Fraud Ring

**Operating mode.** A criminal operation using synthetic media (voice, image, video) and AI-generated text to impersonate executives, family members, advisors, or trusted institutions for financial gain.

**Primary motivations.** Financial extraction; sometimes secondary reputational damage as a by-product.

**Tactics.** Cloned voice authority calls; CEO-impersonation instructions to CFOs and family-office staff; impersonated wealth-manager outreach; synthetic family-distress fraud; fabricated journalist outreach to extract sensitive material.

**Tells.** Pressure for unusual urgency; out-of-band or hard-to-verify channels; small but unfamiliar requests preceding a larger ask; subtle linguistic deviations from the impersonated party's known register.

**Where it shows up.** Executive AI Misuse Risk (primary), Executive Exposure Index (Deepfake Vulnerability and Public Exposure sub-indices), Crisis Escalation Forecast (live or recent incident scenarios).

---

## 4. Investigative Outlet (Adversarial Posture)

**Operating mode.** A legitimate investigative newsroom or freelance investigator who has formed a hostile thesis and is collecting public and (occasionally) leaked material to substantiate it.

**Primary motivations.** Public-interest journalism; career-defining story; genuinely held belief in the thesis.

**Tactics.** Targeted FOI / FOIA requests; cultivated former-employee or former-advisor sources; methodical archive trawling; structured outreach for comment near publication.

**Tells.** Patterned access requests across registries; outreach to known associates; specific document or transaction questions in the right-to-reply phase.

**Where it shows up.** Public Interpretation Analysis (Investigative journalist lens), Narrative Escalation Map (high-confidence pressure points), Crisis Escalation Forecast (24h to 7d windows).

---

## 5. Partisan Outlet

**Operating mode.** An outlet with explicit ideological framing and a pre-existing posture toward the principal's category, sector, or affiliations.

**Primary motivations.** Audience reinforcement; political alignment; downstream pressure on policymakers.

**Tactics.** Pre-formed narratives; selective sourcing; rapid commentary; cross-platform amplification by aligned accounts.

**Tells.** Coverage angle is predictable from the outlet's prior posture; commentary outpaces reporting; story is recycled across affiliated outlets within hours.

**Where it shows up.** Public Interpretation Analysis (Partisan lens, conditional), Narrative Escalation Map (Vector panel — partisan outlets).

---

## 6. Sector Trade and Industry Outlet

**Operating mode.** A specialist outlet covering the principal's sector with deep technical familiarity and direct access to peers, regulators, and competitors.

**Primary motivations.** Authoritative coverage of the sector; long-term reader trust.

**Tactics.** Sourcing from sector peers and regulators; cautious framings; willingness to break technical stories that the general press will then pick up.

**Tells.** Technical accuracy beyond what general press would produce; direct quotes from sector peers or regulators.

**Where it shows up.** Narrative Escalation Map (Vector panel — sector trades), Public Interpretation Analysis (Investor and regulator lenses, indirectly).

---

## 7. Internal Whistleblower

**Operating mode.** A current or former employee, contractor, or advisor who believes they have evidence of wrongdoing and has chosen to disclose externally.

**Primary motivations.** Vindication; perceived ethical obligation; sometimes financial reward (regulatory bounty regimes); occasionally personal grievance.

**Tactics.** Disclosure to investigative journalists, regulators, or whistleblower-platforms; document preservation; sometimes coordinated counsel.

**Tells.** A specific factual story with documentary anchors; outreach to multiple outlets in parallel; counsel cited in early communications.

**Where it shows up.** Crisis Escalation Forecast (high-severity scenarios), Public Interpretation Analysis (Plaintiffs' counsel lens, conditional), Narrative Escalation Map (high-confidence pressure points).

---

## 8. Coordinated Online Campaign

**Operating mode.** Loosely or tightly coordinated networks of social-platform-native accounts driving sustained pressure around a target.

**Primary motivations.** Highly variable — from genuine cause-driven coordination to commercial harassment-for-hire.

**Tactics.** Repeated framings; coordinated reporting and review-bombing; mass-tagging of journalists and regulators; harassment-adjacent behaviour.

**Tells.** Account-creation patterns; identical phrasings across accounts; sudden velocity changes; targeted overlap with the principal's stakeholder accounts.

**Where it shows up.** Narrative Escalation Map (Vector panel — social-platform-native amplifiers), Public Interpretation Analysis (Activist lens, sometimes Hostile Media operator lens).

---

## 9. Opportunistic Litigant

**Operating mode.** An individual, group, or counsel seeking commercial or strategic gain through litigation against a principal or principal's organisation.

**Primary motivations.** Settlement value; precedent value; sometimes activist objectives expressed through litigation.

**Tactics.** Demand letters and pre-action correspondence; coordinated PR around filings; cultivation of supportive media coverage; class-action assembly.

**Tells.** Pre-action correspondence framed for media; coordinated coverage timed with filing; multi-jurisdictional filings.

**Where it shows up.** Crisis Escalation Forecast (24h to 14d windows), Public Interpretation Analysis (Plaintiffs' counsel lens, conditional).

---

## 10. Hostile Acquirer or Activist Investor

**Operating mode.** A financial actor pressuring the principal's organisation through narrative, governance, and capital-market mechanisms.

**Primary motivations.** Strategic or financial gain; governance change; portfolio outcomes.

**Tactics.** Open letters; coordinated analyst coverage; targeted investor outreach; selective leaking; governance proxy fights.

**Tells.** Bespoke microsite content; analyst notes appearing in unusual coordination; pre-positioned proxy advisor support.

**Where it shows up.** Narrative Escalation Map (Vector panel — investor-aligned amplifiers), Crisis Escalation Forecast (governance-themed scenarios), Public Interpretation Analysis (Investor lens).
