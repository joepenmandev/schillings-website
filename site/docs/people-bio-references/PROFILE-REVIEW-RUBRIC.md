# Profile Review Rubric

Use this rubric for Phase 1 cohort reviews.  
Purpose: keep evaluation consistent and prevent subjective drift.

Scoring:

- **2** = clearly meets standard
- **1** = partially meets standard
- **0** = does not meet standard

Suggested threshold for approval: **16/20** minimum, with no zero on questions 1, 2, 6, or 9.

---

## 10 Review Questions

1. **Does authority form before self-description?**  
   Are role, remit, and relevance clear before broad claims?

2. **Is proof discoverable early in the journey?**  
   Can a skim reader find concrete credibility signals without deep scrolling?

3. **Do claims have nearby support?**  
   Are major assertions paired with context, evidence, or operational specifics?

4. **Does the biography imply operational seriousness?**  
   Is there concrete matter/jurisdiction/stakeholder texture without over-disclosure?

5. **Is the writing dense without clutter?**  
   Are paragraphs information-rich, scannable, and functionally distinct?

6. **Does proof remain plain and non-decorative?**  
   Is credibility conveyed without badge theater or prestige decoration?

7. **Does the profile avoid marketing-led rhetoric?**  
   Are superlatives minimized and replaced by specific, verifiable detail?

8. **Is the contact pathway restrained and procedural?**  
   Does CTA/contact language feel calm, institutional, and non-salesy?

9. **Does the profile feel institution-first and system-consistent?**  
   Is it clearly part of the shared practitioner architecture and tone?

10. **Would this profile survive scrutiny from a sophisticated referrer?**  
    Would a high-discretion evaluator find it credible after a quick read?

---

## Cohort Gate Use

- **Cohort 1 (high-profile partners):** establish baseline quality benchmark.
- **Cohort 2 (mid-density profiles):** validate scalability and consistency.
- **Cohort 3 (thin profiles):** apply only after thin-profile policy is proven.

If a cohort repeatedly fails on the same question, pause rollout and update standards before continuing.
